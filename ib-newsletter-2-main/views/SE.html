Social Entrepreneurship

Be the change you wish to see in the world

“The best way to predict the future is to create it.”
- Peter DruckerRedesigning toilets, making websites and revolutionizing education for students with special needs. All of this was part of the Social Entrepreneurship module’s final showcase.

For the past two months, we had critically analyzed and identified numerous issues plaguing our world and upon identifying an appropriate issue we have been working towards solutions for the same.

For the final showcase, we had two main components: a one minute pitch and a one minute video. The videos were a special collaboration between the Exponential Technology segment and the Social Entrepreneurship module. We had a special guest, Mr. Anish Malpani, an alumni of Modern, who judged all of the projects. With over 20 different teams and videos, Mr. Anish had a tough time judging all of them and deciding on the final winners. We also had Dr. Saber judge the one minute advertisement videos of the students and give out the results.
